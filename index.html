My Husband, The Cyborg

In September 2016, actress Susanna Cappellaro was told by her husband that he was going to become a cyborg.

She immediately began filming his radical new path, the impact on their relationship, and her own deep dive into the world of cyborgism - all the while exploring the intersections of technology, love, and what it is to be human.

My Husband, The Cyborg was developed thanks to the BFI Film Fund and has been shooting for over the past three years.

Director: Susanna Cappellaro
Producer: Chiara Ventura, Rubber Stamp Films
Swedish co-producer: Malla Grapengiesser, Fosfor produktion
Associate Producer: Phoebe Hall
Executive Producer: Julia Nottingham, Dorothy Street Pictures
